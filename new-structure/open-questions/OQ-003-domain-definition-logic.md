## Question

What is the principled basis for domains (EFF, HR, Sales)? Project-driven naming cannot remain the rationale.

## Why it matters

Domain is the unit of isolation for resource groups, Foundry instances, billing rollups, and RBAC. A reactive definition undermines all of those.

## Resolution path

- Workshop with business stakeholders to redefine domains on capability or value-stream lines.
- Capture criteria for splitting/merging domains.
